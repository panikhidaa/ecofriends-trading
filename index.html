Who we are

An online studio for businesses that want a digital partner — not a vendor.

Ecofriends Trading OÜ is an online digital studio registered in Tallinn, Estonia. We work entirely remotely with founders, marketing leads and operations managers across the European Union.

We don't chase trends. We turn unclear digital problems into calm, budgeted plans — and then ship them, end to end. From a single landing page to a multi-store e-commerce platform, every project is built on honest scoping, transparent reporting and code we're happy to hand over.

  • Direct work with the people building your project
  • Fixed-scope discovery before any long-term commitment
  • Documentation, ownership and source code always yours
  • Energy- and content-conscious engineering by default
What we do

Six disciplines, one quiet team. Pick what you need — or have us own the full stack.

  1. 01

    Web Design & Development

    Marketing sites, corporate platforms and bespoke web applications built on modern stacks — React, Next.js, TypeScript, WordPress and headless CMS. Fast, accessible, and built to be edited without us in the room.

  2. 02

    E-commerce & Online Stores

    Shopify, WooCommerce and headless commerce setups — from a first store to multi-country, multi-currency rollouts. Payment, shipping, ERP and accounting integrations done properly.

  3. 03

    Brand & Product Design

    Visual identity, design systems and UI/UX for digital products. Calm, deliberate work — wordmarks, type pairings, component libraries and Figma files your team can actually keep using.

  4. 04

    Technical Support & Maintenance

    Monthly retainers with response-time SLAs, security patches, scheduled maintenance windows and a clear ticket pipeline. You ship marketing — we keep production quietly running.

  5. 05

    Performance, SEO & Analytics

    Core Web Vitals audits, technical SEO, lightweight analytics and privacy-friendly tracking. Measurable improvements in load time, search visibility and conversion — without dark patterns.

  6. 06

    Cloud Hosting & DevOps

    Reliable EU-based hosting, CI/CD pipelines, daily encrypted backups and infrastructure-as-code. Sensible architecture for your real-world load — not theoretical hyperscale fantasies.

How we work

Four principles we don't compromise on.

01

Pragmatic over fashionable

We pick the simplest stack that solves your problem for the next three years — not the one with the loudest conference talk this quarter.

02

Designed for the long run

Every project is built to outlive its first launch — clean code, real documentation, and a roadmap your future team can keep extending.

03

Sustainable by default

Lightweight assets, efficient hosting and content modelled for longevity. Less bandwidth, fewer rewrites, lower carbon — and a faster site as a side effect.

04

Transparent, end to end

Weekly demos, written decisions, predictable invoicing. You always know where the project stands — and what the next move costs.

Our process

From first call to quiet, ongoing care.

  1. 01

    Discovery

    A free 45-minute call, followed (if it's a fit) by a short fixed-fee discovery. You leave with a written scope, a clear estimate and an architecture you can show your board.

  2. 02

    Design

    Wireframes, visual direction and a small, opinionated design system. We test the look on real content and real devices — not just pixel-perfect mockups.

  3. 03

    Build

    Two-week sprints, weekly demos, a staging environment from day one. We integrate with your CRM, ERP, payment and email tools as we go — not in a panicked final week.

  4. 04

    Launch & Care

    Soft launch, monitoring, training your team, and an optional support retainer for the quiet years that follow. You keep the code — we keep watch.

Frequently asked

Honest answers, before the call.

What kind of projects do you take on?

Mid-complexity engagements where craft and judgement matter — corporate websites, e-commerce platforms, brand systems, rescue projects and long-term technical partnerships. We avoid throwaway template work and pure body-leasing.

Who do you work with?

We work with SMEs, scale-ups and product teams across the EU — especially in retail, hospitality, B2B services, logistics and digital-first product companies. Everything we do is delivered online, so location is never a blocker.

How does an engagement usually start?

With a short message describing your situation, followed by a free 45-minute call. If it's a fit, we propose a fixed-fee discovery (1–3 weeks) that produces a design direction, an architecture and a clear estimate. Only after that do we discuss long-term delivery.

How do you handle data protection and GDPR?

Every engagement is GDPR-aware from day one: data classification, role separation, encrypted storage, audited backups and signed Data Processing Agreements where applicable. EU-only hosting is the default.

Do you offer ongoing support after launch?

Yes — monthly support retainers with response-time SLAs, scheduled maintenance windows and a clear ticket pipeline. You can also keep the code and host elsewhere; we won't lock you in to make support feel mandatory.

Where are you based and which languages do you work in?

We're a fully online studio registered in Tallinn, Estonia. All projects are delivered remotely across the European Union — there are no offices to visit and no on-site work. Project communication is comfortable in English, Estonian and Russian over email and video calls.
